# Your Life Blueprint: Interactive Experience

An interactive web experience inspired by Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.'s powerful speech "What Is Your Life's Blueprint?" delivered to students at Barratt Junior High School in Philadelphia, PA (1967).

## ğŸ¯ About

This project transforms Dr. King's timeless message into an engaging, interactive experience that helps users create their own life blueprint based on three foundational principles:

1. **Dignity, Worth, & Somebodiness** - Believing deeply in your own inherent value
2. **The Determination to Achieve Excellence** - Striving for mastery in all endeavors
3. **Eternal Principles & Movement** - Committing to beauty, love, justice, and forward progress

## âœ¨ Features

- **Interactive Infographic** - Explore Dr. King's three principles with visual storytelling
- **Interactive Charts** - Dynamic radar and doughnut charts using Chart.js
- **Blueprint Drafting Table** - Create your personal life blueprint
- **Responsive Design** - Beautiful, modern UI that works on all devices
- **YouTube Integration** - Embedded video of Dr. King's original speech
- **Print Functionality** - Save and print your completed blueprint
